FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about hosting a Bitcoin ATM through Imprint Logistics.

What is a Bitcoin ATM (BTM)?+
A Bitcoin ATM (or BTM) is a kiosk that allows customers to buy Bitcoin and other cryptocurrencies using cash. It works similarly to a traditional ATM, but instead of dispensing cash, it sends Bitcoin directly to the customer's digital wallet. BTMs are typically found in convenience stores, gas stations, and other high-traffic retail locations.
How much can I earn hosting a BTM?+
Revenue varies based on location, foot traffic, and transaction volume. With our partners like Bitcoin Depot, hosts can earn up to $7,900 per month per machine through residual revenue sharing. Most locations begin generating income within the first month of activation.
What type of locations qualify?+
Ideal locations include convenience stores, gas stations, smoke shops, laundromats, check cashing stores, grocery stores, liquor stores, and any retail location with high foot traffic and long operating hours. We evaluate each location based on demographics, traffic patterns, and revenue potential before placement.
Who handles installation and maintenance?+
Everything is handled for you. Our operator partners manage shipping, professional installation, cash logistics, software updates, and 24/7 technical monitoring. As a host, you have zero management responsibilities — it's truly passive income.
Is this legal and compliant?+
Absolutely. All our operator partners — Bitcoin Depot, RockItCoin, and Unbank — are registered money services businesses with FINCEN and hold money transmission licenses in all required states. They comply with all applicable federal, state, and local regulations including KYC/AML requirements.
How long does setup take?+
Our typical deployment timeline is under 14 days from signed Letter of Authorization (LOA) to live machine. This includes site assessment, shipping, professional installation, and activation. Some locations can go live even faster.
What does the BTM look like? How much space does it need?+
A standard Bitcoin ATM kiosk takes up roughly 2-3 square feet of floor space — about the size of a small vending machine. It requires a standard electrical outlet and an internet connection (Wi-Fi or Ethernet). The kiosks have a modern, professional appearance that fits well in any retail environment.
Do I need any special permits or licenses?+
As a host location, you generally do not need any special permits or licenses. The BTM operator holds all necessary money transmission licenses and regulatory registrations. You simply provide the physical space and electricity. Our team handles all compliance requirements on the operator side.
Which operators does Imprint Logistics represent?+
We are authorized distributors for three leading BTM operators: Bitcoin Depot (the largest BTM network in North America, NASDAQ: BTM), RockItCoin (one of the fastest-growing operators), and Unbank (a premium BTM provider). We match the right operator to your location based on market dynamics and revenue potential.
Can I host multiple BTMs at different locations?+
Yes! Many of our partners host BTMs at multiple locations. Whether you own a single store or a multi-location chain, we can scale with you. We offer dedicated account management for multi-site partners to ensure consistent deployment and revenue optimization.
Do you serve locations outside the United States?+
Yes. While our primary market is the United States, we are actively expanding into Canada and Brazil. If you have locations in these markets, we'd love to discuss placement opportunities. Contact us for more details on international availability.
How do I get started?+
Simply reach out to us through our Contact page, email us at info@imprintlogistics.com, or message us on WhatsApp. Share your location details, and our team will assess eligibility, recommend the best operator, and get you on the path to earning within days.
